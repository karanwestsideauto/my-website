WHAT IS A STARLIGHT HEADLINER?

Starlight headliners have been made popular with brands like Rolls-Royce and give the ceiling of your car the magical illusion of a star-filled sky using thousands of fiber-optic lights. We carefully remove the existing headliner from your vehicle and hand-poked thousands of incredibly tiny openings and then individually set a fiber optic light through each and every single perforation. We then hard wire the light engine to your vehicle and re-install the headliner into your vehicle for a perfect and seamless installation that looks and feels exactly like it came out of the Rolls-Royce factory.
